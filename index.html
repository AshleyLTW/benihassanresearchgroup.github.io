This website aims to provide a visual dictionary for all inscribed tombs at Beni Hassan, which draws on published and unpublished material collected by the Macquarie University expedition working at Beni Hassan (2009-present). The Beni Hassan Visual Dictionary contains accessible descriptions of the various themes, scenes, details and architectural features in a tomb, Jsesh transcriptions, transliteration, and translation of the hieroglyphic inscriptions, in addition to high resolution zoomable photographs and line drawings.

The Beni Hassan @ Macquarie Project is supported by an Australian Research Council Discovery Project grant from 2016--2018, and is a collaboration between Macquarie University and the Metropolitan Museum of Art, New York.
